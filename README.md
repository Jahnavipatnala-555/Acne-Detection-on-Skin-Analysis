# Dermalyze: AI-Powered Acne Detection and Skin Analysis Platform

## Overview

Dermalyze is an AI-powered skin analysis platform designed to help users understand their skin condition through intelligent image analysis and personalized skincare guidance.

The platform aims to combine computer vision, skin profiling, recommendation systems, and conversational AI to create a personalized skin-health experience. Users can upload facial images, receive skin assessments, and access customized skincare recommendations tailored to their individual concerns.

---

## Problem Statement

Many individuals struggle to identify skin concerns accurately and often receive generic skincare advice that may not suit their skin type, lifestyle, or specific needs.

Dermalyze addresses this challenge by providing an intelligent platform capable of analyzing visible skin concerns and generating personalized recommendations to support informed skincare decisions.
